# 🎮 Video Game Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes global video game sales across genres, platforms, publishers, and years.

The dashboard provides insights into industry trends, top-performing games, dominant platforms, and leading publishers to understand how the gaming market has evolved over time.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

* Analyze global sales distribution by genre
* Identify top-selling games
* Compare platform performance
* Evaluate publisher dominance
* Understand sales trends over time (1980–2020)

---

## 🗂 Dataset Information

Dataset Used: `vgsales.csv`

The dataset contains:

* Game Name
* Platform
* Year of Release
* Genre
* Publisher
* Regional Sales (NA, EU, JP, Other)
* Global Sales

This is a structured transactional dataset used for direct visualization modeling in Tableau.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components & Insights

### 🔹 Total Sales by Genre

Top Genres by Global Sales:

* Action (Highest)
* Sports
* Shooter
* Role-Playing
* Platform

📌 Action games dominate global sales.

### 🔹 Sales Trend by Year & Genre (1980–2020)

* Rapid growth from mid-1990s
* Peak around 2008–2010
* Decline post-2015

📌 Golden era of physical game sales occurred between 2005–2010.


### 🔹 Top 10 Games by Sales

Top-selling titles:

* Wii Sports
* Grand Theft Auto V
* Super Mario Bros
* Tetris
* Mario Kart Wii

📌 Nintendo titles dominate historical best-sellers.

### 🔹 Top Platforms by Sales

Leading platforms:

* PS2 (Highest)
* X360
* PS3
* Wii
* DS

📌 Console gaming platforms dominated the market during peak years.

### 🔹 Top Publishers by Sales

Leading publishers:

* Nintendo
* Electronic Arts
* Activision
* Sony Computer Entertainment
* Ubisoft

📌 Nintendo leads both in titles and global revenue.

### 🔹 Dataset Summary Metrics

* Total Games: 11,493
* Total Genres: 12
* Total Platforms: 31
* Total Publishers: 579
* Data Range: 1980 – 2020

## 💡 Key Business Insights

1. Action genre generates highest global revenue.
2. Gaming industry experienced exponential growth during 2005–2010.
3. PS2 is the most successful platform historically.
4. Nintendo is the leading publisher in global sales.
5. Console dominance defined the industry before digital transformation.
