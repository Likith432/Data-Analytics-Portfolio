# 🎬 Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes Netflix’s global content library to uncover insights into content distribution, genre trends, ratings, and country-level production patterns.

The dashboard provides an interactive exploration of Movies and TV Shows based on release year, rating, genre, and geographic distribution.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

* Analyze Netflix’s content growth over time
* Compare Movies vs TV Shows distribution
* Identify top-performing genres
* Understand rating distribution
* Explore country-wise content production
* Provide strategic insights into content expansion trends

---

## 🗂 Dataset Information

Dataset Used: `netflix_titles.csv`

The dataset includes:

* Title
* Type (Movie / TV Show)
* Rating
* Release Year
* Duration
* Date Added
* Country
* Genre (Listed In)
* Description

This is a structured single dataset used for direct visualization modeling in Tableau.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components & Insights

### 🔹 Movies vs TV Shows Distribution

* Movies: 4,265 (68%)
* TV Shows: 1,969 (32%)

📌 Netflix’s content library is dominated by Movies.

### 🔹 Ratings Distribution

Top ratings observed:

* TV-MA (Highest)
* TV-14
* TV-PG
* R
* PG-13

📌 Indicates strong focus on mature and teen audiences.

### 🔹 Top 10 Genres

Top genres include:

* Documentaries
* Stand-Up Comedy
* Dramas (International)
* Kids TV
* International Movies

📌 High volume of documentary and international content reflects global strategy.

### 🔹 Content by Country

* USA contributes the highest number of titles.
* India and other international markets also show strong representation.

📌 Netflix has strong global content diversification.

### 🔹 Content Growth by Year

* Significant growth after 2015.
* Peak additions between 2017–2019.
* Rapid expansion phase during that period.

📌 Reflects Netflix’s aggressive content acquisition strategy.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

1. Movies dominate the Netflix content library.
2. Majority content targeted toward mature audiences (TV-MA, TV-14).
3. Content growth accelerated post-2015.
4. USA leads content production, but international presence is strong.
5. Documentaries and international dramas are highly represented.
